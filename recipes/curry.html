Japanese Curry

Description

Delicious Japanese chicken curry recipe for a weeknight dinner! Tender pieces of chicken, carrots, and potatoes cooked in a rich savory curry sauce, this Japanese version of curry is a must-have for your family meal.

Ingredients

  • 1½ lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs (you can also use beef, pork, seafood, tofu, mushrooms, or more vegetables)
  • ¼ tsp kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 onions
  • 3 yukon gold potatoes
  • 1 knob ginger
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • ½ apple
  • 1½ Tbsp neutral-flavored oil

For the curry

  • 4 cups Chicken Stock/Broth (homemade or store bought)
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp ketchup
  • 1 package Japanese curry roux
  • 2-4 tsp kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare ingredients by cutting chicken, onions, carrots, apples, and potatoes. Grate ginger and apple.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat and add onions. Cook until translucent.
  3. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, mix well together.
  4. Add chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  5. Add chicken stock, grated apple, honey, soy sauce, and ketchup.
  6. Add carrots and potatoes.
  7. Simmer while covered on medium-low heat for 15 minutes. Or until carrots and potatoes are cooked through.
  8. Turn off heat and add roux
  9. Simmer, uncovered, until curry becomes thick.
  10. Serve with rice
